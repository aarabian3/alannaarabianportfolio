About Me

I am currently a senior at Elon University pursuing a bachelor of the arts degree in strategic communications. I am a highly creative individual with extensive experience in writing, podcast production, and social media management. I am actively involved outside of class and hold multiple executive positions. I am passionate about anxiety disorder awareness and hope to combine my strategic communications degree with providing mental health resources, especially for children. On the side, I enjoy spending quality time with my friends and family. Introverted and "wise beyond my years" as my mom likes to say, I love to write, read, and learn new things. On the weekend you can find me either at the local coffee shop, at the bookstore buying another book I don't quite need yet, or attending art galleries with friends.
