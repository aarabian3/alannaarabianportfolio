My Work

Alanna's Journal is a wellness and self-help blog. The website is updated with content monthly and topics have covered personal growth reflections and techniques, anxiety remedies and hacks, study skills and learning habits, and more.

The 3 a.m. is a podcast I created as well as host and produce to coincide with Alanna's Journal. It is called "3 a.m." because it is devoted to all of the late night thoughts and struggles we all go through. Each episode has a mental health or wellness topic like stress, worry, anxiety, and self-image, that I talk about and I also provide tools and coping skills.
